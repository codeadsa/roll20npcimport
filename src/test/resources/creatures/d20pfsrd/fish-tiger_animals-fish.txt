Tiger Fish CR 1
XP 400
N Medium animal (aquatic)
Init +2; Senses low-light vision; Perception +8
DEFENSE AC 13, touch 12, flat-footed 11 (+2 Dex, +1 natural)
hp 16 (3d8+3)
Fort +4, Ref +5, Will +2
OFFENSE Speed swim 50 ft.
Melee bite +4 (1d6+3)
Special Attacks interlocking bite
STATISTICS Str 15, Dex 14, Con 13, Int 1, Wis 12, Cha 2
Base Atk +2; CMB +4 (+8 grapple); CMD 16
Feats Skill Focus (Perception), Skill Focus (Swim)
Skills Perception +8, Stealth +6, Swim +17
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Interlocking Bite (Ex) A tiger fish gains a +2 bonus on all drag combat maneuvers and can initiate a drag combat maneuver whenever it scores a successful bite attack against a target. A tiger fish does not provoke attacks of opportunity when initiating a drag maneuver.
