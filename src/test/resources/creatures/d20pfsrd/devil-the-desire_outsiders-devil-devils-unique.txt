hp 56 (7d8+21)

OFFENSE
Speed 30 ft.
Melee mwk envenomed dagger +6 (1d4+1 + giant wasp poison)

Spell-Like Abilities (CL 7th)

STATISTICS

SQ bardic abilities, beauty, honeyed words
Gear amulet of natural armor (+1), bracers of armor +3, other items as given by her lovers or acquired
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Bardic Abilities The Desire is a 7th level bard with the versatile performances of act and dance.
Beauty (Ex) The Desire is exceptionally alluring, possessing unearthly beauty and charm, she gains a +2 circumstance bonus to all social rolls against those who find her attractive. When such a person would attack her, they must make a Will save (DC 19) or suffer a -1 moral penalty to all of their attack against her for the duration of the encounter.
Desire’s Gift (Su) The Desire has full control over the magic items known as Desire’s Gifts, see the item for more details.
Honeyed Words (Su) Given time, the Desire can worm her way into almost any heart. If she spends at least an hour a day in the company of a person for a week, the Desire gains a +4 competence bonus on all social skills and +2 to all of her abilities’ DC against that person for the next month.
Lover’s Guardian (Ex) For each adjacent ally, the Desire gains a +1 circumstance bonus to armor class up to a maximum of +4 as they constantly move to protect her.
Lover’s Sacrifice (Su) Once per round, as a free action, The Desire may switch places with one of her allies within 10′.
Associated Magic Item: Desire’s Gift
