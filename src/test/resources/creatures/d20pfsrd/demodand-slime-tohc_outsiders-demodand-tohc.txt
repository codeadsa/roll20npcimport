Slime Demodand CR 13

CE Medium outsider (chaotic, demodand, evil, extraplanar)
Init +4; Senses darkvision 120 ft.; Perception +22; Aura stench (DC 21, 2d6 rounds)
DEFENSE
AC 28, touch 14, flat-footed 24 (+4 Dex, +14 natural)
hp 136 (13d10+65)
Fort +13, Ref +8, Will +10
DR 10/good and magic; Immune acid, poison, mind-affecting effects; Resist cold 10, fire 10; SR 24
OFFENSE
Speed 40 ft., fly 50 ft. (average)
Melee 2 claws +22 (1d6+8 plus 1d6 acid), bite +21 (1d8+8 plus 1d6 acid)
Special Attacks enslime

At will—detect good, detect magic, detect thoughts (DC 16), fear (DC 18), gaseous form, invisibility (self only), magic circle against good, see invisibility, tongues
3/day—acid fog, dispel magic, fog cloud, ray of enfeeblement
1/day—summon (level 6, 1d4 tarry demodands 50%; or 1 slime demodand 20%)
STATISTICS

Languages Abyssal, Common, Infernal
SQ slimy hide
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Enslime (Ex) If a slime demodand hits an opponent with all of its natural attacks in the same round, it deals an extra 1d6 points of acid damage (in addition to the acid damage dealt by its natural attacks).
Slimy Hide (Ex) A slime demodand’s hide is covered in acidic slime. Creatures attacking it unarmed or with natural weapons take 1d6 points of acid damage each time one of their attacks hits.
