SPECIAL ABILITIES
Attach (Ex) A dire weasel automatically latches onto its target with a successful bite attack. The dire weasel is considered grappling, but the target is not. The target can attack or grapple the dire weasel as normal or, alternatively, break the grapple with a successful grapple or Escape Artist check. A dire weasel has a +8 racial bonus to maintain a grapple.
Blood Drain (Ex) Dire weasels drain blood each round they remain attached, inflicting 1 point of Strength and Constitution damage.
