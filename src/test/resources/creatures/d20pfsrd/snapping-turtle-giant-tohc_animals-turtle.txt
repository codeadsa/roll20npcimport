Giant Snapping Turtle CR 10
XP 9,600
N Gargantuan animal (aquatic)
Init +2; Senses low-light vision, scent; Perception +20
DEFENSE
AC 20, touch 4, flat-footed 20 (–2 Dex, +16 natural, –4 size)
hp 150 (12d8+84 plus 12)
Fort +15, Ref +6, Will +7
OFFENSE
Speed 10 ft., swim 20 ft.
Melee bite +16 (2d10+16/19–20 plus grab)
Space 20 ft.; Reach 15 ft.
Special Attacks swallow whole (2d8+11 acid damage, AC 18, 15 hp)
TACTICS
A giant snapping turtle lurks near the shore or on the bottom of a body of water, where it remains motionless and hidden in its shell. When prey passes near, it shoots its neck out and bites.

Str 33, Dex 6, Con 25, Int 2, Wis 13, Cha 6

Skills Perception +20, Swim +24
SQ amphibious
