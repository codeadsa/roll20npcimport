Space 10 ft.; Reach 5 ft.

SPECIAL ABILITIES
Disease (Su) Ghoul Fever Bite—injury; save Fort DC 16; onset 1 day; frequency 1/day; effect 1d3 Con and 1d3 Dex damage; cure 2 consecutive saves.
The save DC is Charisma-based. A humanoid who dies of ghoul fever rises as a ghoul at the next midnight.
Screech (Su) Once per day as a standard action, a skaveling can screech as a mobat, affecting a 20-foot-radius burst; those who are affected are stunned for 1d3 rounds unless they make a DC 16 Fortitude save. The save DC is Charisma-based.
