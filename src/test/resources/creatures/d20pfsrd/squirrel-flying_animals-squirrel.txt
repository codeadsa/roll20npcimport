OFFENSE
Speed 20 ft., fly 40 ft. (clumsy)
Melee bite +4 (1d3-4)
Space 2-1/2 ft.; Reach 0 ft.

Str 3, Dex 15, Con 8, Int 1, Wis 12, Cha 6

Familiar The master of a flying squirrel familiar gains a +3 bonus on Fly checks.
