SPECIAL ABILITIES
Breath Stealing (Su) A slinker can suck the air from a living victim’s lungs as a full-round action against any helpless opponent. The victim must succeed on a DC 11 Fortitude save. If the save fails, it takes 1d4 points of Constitution damage. At Con 0, the victim dies. The save DC is Constitution-based.
