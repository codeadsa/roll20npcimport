Tendrul CR 7
XP 3,200
N(E) Large aberration (aquatic)
Init +2; Senses darkvision 60 ft., keen scent; Perception +11
DEFENSE
AC 21, touch 12, flat-footed 18 (+2 Dex, +1 dodge, +9 natural, -1 size)
hp 76 (9d8+27 plus 9)
Fort +6, Ref +5, Will +9
OFFENSE
Speed 10 ft.; swim 40 ft.
Melee bite +10 (1d8+5/x3 plus grab), tail slap +5 (1d6+2)
Space 10 ft.; Reach 5 ft.
Special Attacks constrict (1d8+5), gnashing of teeth
STATISTICS
Str 21, Dex 15, Con 17, Int 3, Wis 12, Cha 6
Base Atk +6; CMB +12 (+16 grapple); CMD 24
Feats Improved Iron Will, Iron Will, Power Attack, Skill Focus (Perception), Toughness
Skills Perception +11, Swim +16
SQ amphibious
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Gnashing of Teeth (Ex) If a tendrul scores a natural 20 on its attack roll with its bite attack and subsequently confirms the critical hit, it deals triple normal damage.
Keen Scent (Ex) A tendrul notices creatures by scent in a 90-foot radius and detects blood in the water at ranges of up to 500 feet.
