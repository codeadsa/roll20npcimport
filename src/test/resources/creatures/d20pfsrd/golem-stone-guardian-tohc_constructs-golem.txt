SPECIAL ABILITIES
Light Fortification (Ex) A stone guardian has a 25% chance to ignore additional damage caused by critical hits or sneak attacks.
Ring Link (Su) A stone guardian is linked to a magic ring. The construct will never attack any creature that wears this ring, nor any creatures within 10 feet of the ringwearer, unless one of those creatures attacks the guardian first.
See Invisibility (Ex) A stone guardian constantly sees invisible creatures and objects, as per the spell see invisibility.
