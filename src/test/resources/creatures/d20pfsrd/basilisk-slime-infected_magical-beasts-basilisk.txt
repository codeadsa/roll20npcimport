Slime-Infected Basilisk CR 6
XP 2,400
Variant advanced basilisk
N Medium magical beast (aquatic)
Init +1; Senses darkvision 60 ft., low-light vision; Perception +12
DEFENSE
AC 21, touch 11, flat-footed 20 (+1 Dex, +10 natural)
hp 52 (7d10+14)
Fort +9, Ref +6, Will +7
OFFENSE
Speed 20 ft., swim 30 ft.
Melee bite +12 (1d8+7)
Special Attacks gaze (30 ft., DC 15)
TACTICS
Before Combat The slime-infected basilisk depends on its transparent skin to help it surprise unsuspecting creatures.
During Combat The basilisk ignores opponents petrified by its gaze instead biting those still resisting.
Morale The basilisk retreats when reduced to fewer than 13 hit points.
STATISTICS
Str 20, Dex 12, Con 15, Int 2, Wis 17, Cha 15
Base Atk +7; CMB +12; CMD 23 (35 vs. trip)
Feats Blind-Fight, Great Fortitude, Iron Will, Skill Focus (Perception)
Skills Perception +12, Stealth +12 (+16 underwater), Swim +13; Racial Modifiers +4 Stealth (+8 underwater)
