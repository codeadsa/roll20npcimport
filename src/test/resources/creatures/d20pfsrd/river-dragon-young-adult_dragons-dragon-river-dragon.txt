Young Adult River Dragon CR 10
XP 9,600
CN Large dragon (aquatic, fire)
Init +5; Senses dragon senses, watersight; Perception +22; Aura frightful presence (150 ft., DC 18)
DEFENSE
AC 26, touch 10, flat-footed 25 (+1 Dex, +16 natural, -1 size)
hp 126 (12d12+48)
Fort +12, Ref +9, Will +11
DR 5/magic; Immune fire, dragon traits; SR 21
Weaknesses vulnerability to cold
OFFENSE
Speed 30 ft., swim 60 ft.
Melee bite +17 (2d6+9), tail +17 (1d8+9)
Space 10 ft.; Reach 5 ft. (10 ft with bite)
Special Attacks breath weapon (80-ft. line, DC 20, 10d6 fire)
Spell-Like Abilities (CL 12th; concentration +14)
At will— obscuring mist, soften earth and stone

Sorcerer Spells Known (CL 3rd; concentration +5)
1st (5/day)— expeditious retreat, identify, reduce person
0 (at will)— detect magic, ghost sound, mage hand, message, prestidigitation
STATISTICS
Str 23 Dex 13, Con 18, Int 13, Wis 16, Cha 14
Base Atk +12; CMB +19; CMD 30 (cannot be tripped)
Feats Alertness, Athletic, Cleave, Great Cleave, Improved Initiative, Power Attack
Skills Intimidate +17, Knowledge (nature) +16, Perception +22, Spellcraft +16, Stealth +12, Survival, +18, Swim +33; Racial Modifiers +8 Swim
Languages Common, Draconic
SQ air breathing, speak with aquatic, watersight
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Air Breathing (Ex) The river dragon can breathe air just as naturally as water. This dragon can freely use its breath weapon, spells and other abilities in both air and water.
Speak with Aquatic (Ex) This river dragon gains a constant spell-like ability to speak with animals of the aquatic subtype. This otherwise functions as speak with animals.
Watersight (Ex) This river dragon can see underwater four times better than a human.
