Errata?
Incorporeal creatures get a deflection bonus to AC equal to their Charisma bonus (minimum +1). A Lesser Shadow has Charisma 14 which equals a +2 bonus, therefore the correct bonus should be +2 not +1. GMs are free to use discretion when determining which bonus to use however.
