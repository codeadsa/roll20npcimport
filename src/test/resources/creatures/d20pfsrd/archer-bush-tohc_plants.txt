Archer Bush CR 2
XP 600
N Small plant
Init +0; Senses tremorsense 60 ft.; Perception +0
DEFENSE
AC 14, touch 11, flat-footed 14 (+3 natural, +1 size)
hp 21 (3d8+9)
Fort +6; Ref +1; Will +1
Immune plant traits
OFFENSE
Speed 10 ft.
Ranged thorns +4 (1d8)
TACTICS
During Combat An archer bush attacks by firing a cluster of thorns at any creature that comes within 20 feet of its location.
STATISTICS
Str 11, Dex 10, Con 16, Int 2, Wis 11, Cha 9
Base Atk +2; CMB +1; CMD 11 (can’t be tripped)
Feats Skill Focus (Stealth), Weapon Focus (thorns)
Skills Stealth +13
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Thorns (Ex) An archer bush can loose a cluster of needlesharp thorns at a single target within 20 feet as a standard action. An opponent hit by the thorns takes 1d8 points of piercing damage. Further, the target takes a –1 circumstance penalty on attack rolls, saves, and checks until the thorns are removed (full-round action to remove the thorns).
