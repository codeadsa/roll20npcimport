Spit (Ex) A spitting cobra can spit its venom into the eyes of its opponent as a ranged touch attack (+5 to hit). A creature hit by the venom must make Fortitude saves to resist the poison as if bitten (see above), and must also succeed a DC 10 Reflex save or be blinded. The save DC for this ability is Constitution-based.
