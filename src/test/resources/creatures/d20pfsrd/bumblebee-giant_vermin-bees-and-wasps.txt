Giant Bumblebee CR 2
XP 600
N Medium vermin
Init +4; Senses darkvision 60 ft.; Perception +3
DEFENSE
AC 17, touch 14, flat-footed 13 (+4 Dex, +3 natural)
hp 22 (3d8+9)
Fort +6, Ref +5, Will +4
Immune mind-affecting effects
Weaknesses vulnerable to smoke
OFFENSE
Speed 20 ft., fly 60 ft. (good)
Melee sting +4 (1d4+3 plus poison)
STATISTICS
Str 15, Dex 18, Con 17, Int –, Wis 16, Cha 13
Base Atk +2; CMB +4; CMD 18 (26 vs. trip)
Skills Fly +8
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Poison (Ex) Sting—injury; save Fort DC 14; frequency 1/round for 4 rounds; effect 1d2 Con; cure 1 save.
Vulnerable to Smoke (Ex) Smoke from particularly smoky fires or effects (such as that created by a pyrotechnics spell) causes a giant bee to become nauseated if it fails a DC 14 Fortitude save. This condition persists as long as the giant bumblebee remains in the smoke, plus 1d4 rounds.
