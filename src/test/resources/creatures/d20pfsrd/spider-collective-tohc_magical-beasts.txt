Spider Collective
CR 2
XP 600
N Medium magical beast
Init +6; Senses darkvision 60 ft., low-light vision, tremorsense 30 ft.; Perception +9
DEFENSE
AC 16, touch 12, flat-footed 14 (+2 Dex, +4 natural)
hp 13 (2d10+2)
Fort +4; Ref +5; Will +0
Defensive Abilities one from many; DR 5/bludgeoning; Immune collective immunities
Weaknesses collective vulnerabilities
OFFENSE
Speed 30 ft., climb 20 ft.
Melee bite +4 (1d6+3 plus poison)
Special Attacks engulf, many from one, poison, swarming body
STATISTICS
Str 14, Dex 15, Con 12, Int 6, Wis 10, Cha 11
Base Atk +2; CMB +4; CMD 16 (can’t be tripped)
Feats Improved Initiative
Skills Climb +10, Perception +9; Racial Modifiers +4 Perception
SQ collective traits
SPECIAL ABILITIES
Engulf (Ex) Reflex DC 12. An engulfed creature is subject a spider collective’s poison (see below) while engulfed.
Many from One (Ex) Attack bonus +4 ranged, damage 1d6. A creature struck is subjected to the spider collective’s poison.
Poison (Ex) Bite—Injury; save Fort DC 12; frequency 1/round for 2 rounds; effect 1d2 Str; cure 1 save.
Swarming Body (Ex) Any creature attacking a spider collective unarmed or with natural weapons automatically takes 1d4 points of damage plus poison damage.
